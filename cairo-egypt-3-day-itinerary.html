3 Days in Cairo: A Complete Day-by-Day Itinerary

Exactly how to spend a perfect 3-day trip to Cairo — what to see, where to stay, and how to get around.

Cairo rewards a plan. This 3-day itinerary groups the city by area so you spend your time seeing Cairo rather than crossing it — with a complete day-by-day schedule below, plus where to stay, eat and get around for every budget.

Day 1 Journey Through Antiquity: Pyramids & Pharaohs

09:00 AM

Explore the Giza Pyramids and Sphinx

Begin your day with a private guided tour of the iconic Giza Plateau, home to the Great Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx. Marvel at these ancient wonders, learn about their history and construction, and capture breathtaking photos. Consider an optional camel ride for a classic desert experience.

01:00 PM

Gourmet Lunch with Pyramid Views

Indulge in a luxurious lunch at a restaurant offering unparalleled panoramic views of the Giza Pyramids, perhaps from an exclusive terrace. Savor a blend of international and Egyptian delicacies in an elegant setting.

03:00 PM

Discover Saqqara and the Step Pyramid

Journey to Saqqara, an ancient burial ground serving as the necropolis for the ancient Egyptian capital, Memphis. Explore the Step Pyramid of Djoser, considered the world's first stone building, and visit the Imhotep Museum and various mastaba tombs showcasing intricate hieroglyphics.

07:30 PM

Nile River Dinner Cruise with Entertainment

Conclude your day with a magical evening on a luxury Nile River dinner cruise. Enjoy a sumptuous buffet or à la carte dinner, live entertainment including traditional belly dancing and Sufi whirling dervishes, all while cruising past Cairo's illuminated skyline.

Day 2 Grandeur of Museums & Charms of Islamic Cairo

09:00 AM

Immersive Visit to the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM)

Spend your morning exploring the magnificent Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM), an architectural marvel housing an unparalleled collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the complete Tutankhamun collection. Allow ample time to fully appreciate this world-class institution.

01:00 PM

Upscale Egyptian Lunch in Zamalek

Cross over to Zamalek Island for a gourmet lunch experience at a highly-rated restaurant specializing in refined Egyptian cuisine. Enjoy traditional flavors in a sophisticated, contemporary setting popular with locals and expats.

03:00 PM

Guided Exploration of Khan el-Khalili and Islamic Cairo

Embark on a private guided tour through the labyrinthine alleys of Khan el-Khalili bazaar. Beyond souvenir shops, discover hidden artisan workshops, historic cafes, and magnificent Islamic architecture. Visit nearby Al-Azhar Mosque and the El-Moez Street for a deeper dive into Cairo's rich Islamic heritage.

07:30 PM

Exclusive Dining Experience with Nile Views

Enjoy an evening of exquisite fine dining at one of Zamalek's most exclusive establishments, many of which offer stunning views of the Nile. Savor gourmet cuisine, curated wine selections, and impeccable service in an elegant ambiance.

Day 3 Coptic Heritage & Citadel Majesty

09:00 AM

Journey Through Coptic Cairo's Sacred Sites

Explore the historic heart of Coptic Cairo with a private guide. Visit the Hanging Church (Saint Virgin Mary's Coptic Orthodox Church), one of the oldest churches in Egypt, the Abu Serga Church (Cavern Church), and the Ben Ezra Synagogue. Immerse yourself in the rich religious history of the area.

01:00 PM

Refined Lunch in Garden City

Enjoy a sophisticated lunch in the upscale Garden City district, known for its elegant ambiance and diplomatic residences. Choose from a selection of fine restaurants offering international or contemporary Egyptian cuisine in a serene setting.

03:00 PM

Ascend to the Citadel of Salah al-Din and Muhammad Ali Mosque

Visit the majestic Citadel of Salah al-Din, a medieval Islamic fortress offering panoramic views of Cairo. Explore the stunning Muhammad Ali Mosque, an Ottoman-era mosque with its distinctive alabaster exterior and grand interior, symbolizing Egyptian independence.

07:30 PM

Farewell Dinner with Panoramic City Views at Cairo Tower

Conclude your Cairo journey with a memorable farewell dinner at the revolving restaurant atop the Cairo Tower. Enjoy a exquisite meal and unparalleled 360-degree panoramic views of the entire city, beautifully illuminated at night, offering a perfect end to your luxury experience.

Where to stay, eat & get around

Cairo works on every budget. Here is how it breaks down across three tiers.

🛌 Where to stay

Budget: Downtown Cairo (especially around Talaat Harb or Tahrir Square for essential but central stays), Ramses Square area (for strong transport links and local markets).
Mid-range: Garden City (serene, diplomatic district with elegant boutique hotels), Dokki (residential, vibrant local life, well-connected to major attractions), parts of Mohandessin.
Luxury: Zamalek Island (Cairo's most affluent and artistic district with high-end boutiques and Nile views), Nile Corniche (prime riverside locations boasting international luxury hotel brands), select exclusive resorts near the Giza Pyramids for unparalleled ancient views.

🚉 Getting around

Budget: Cairo Metro (most efficient for specific routes downtown and across the Nile), local microbuses/minivans (for adventurous travelers familiar with local routes).
Mid-range: Uber/Careem (reliable ridesharing apps offering a good balance of cost and convenience), combining Cairo Metro for longer distances with ridesharing for last-mile access.
Luxury: Private chauffeured luxury sedans (pre-booked for door-to-door comfort), dedicated hotel limousine services, specialized tour vehicles with professional drivers and multilingual guides.

🍽 Where to eat

Budget: Khan el-Khalili bazaar (authentic street food, local cafes offering falafel and koshary), Downtown Cairo (Tahrir area for famous local eateries like Abou Tarek for Koshary), local neighborhood spots in Dokki or Mohandessin.
Mid-range: Zamalek (diverse array of trendy bistros, international and refined Egyptian cuisine), Garden City (upscale cafes and international dining), selected restaurants in Downtown Cairo offering elevated local dishes in stylish settings.
Luxury: Nile-side fine dining restaurants (particularly in Zamalek and along the Corniche for exquisite views and international menus), exclusive hotel restaurants known for their culinary innovation and ambiance, upscale dining establishments in Heliopolis or Katameya offering sophisticated experiences.
